The Insight

80% of girls drop out of sport by age 14. Not because they want to. Because the world tells them they don't belong. Girls are hearing it through rosters, coaching culture, body shaming, media silence. And then it calls it a choice. This isn't attrition. It's exclusion.

The Strategy

This campaign is built around a wordplay system that embeds HER into the tagline. The iconic Swoosh is rendered in purple, signifying this cause as distinct and deliberate. Strong imagery of girls in sport stops viewers cold, exposes injustice, and leaves something impossible to ignore.
